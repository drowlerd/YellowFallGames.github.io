Roadmap

To the infinity and beyond!

  • September 2019

    The Flame Initiative

    The idea was to bring together a group of remarkable people, see if they could become something more. See if they could work together when to make the games they've always dreamed of.

  • January 2020

    Ripáralo Claudio

    The team headed to the gates of our first Game Jam. We've barely opened a game engine before, but we managed to deliver a game in 3 days. The premise ws simple: To Have Fun Doing Games. We learned to deal with our flaws and comunicate with each other

  • September 2020

    Dab Spanker

    We tried to make a game following the same principle we stood in the beginning: To Have Fun Doing Games. That's how we managed to compete with other 130 games. We learned to get the most out of feedback and social media campaign in 5 days, 28th place.

  • February 2021

    The Sticky Plan

    As we listened to the last project feedback, we prepared for the battle again. We analized and organized our strengths and duties. 7 days of pain and glory led us to Sticky Plan, a multi award winner, but over it, to work as a true team.

  • March 2021

    The Flame and the Crystal

    We wanted to try something new, a big deal: an isometric game with story and puzzles. We worked splitted, half of the team working at the same time on the project. We learned about how we need each other and how easy we blend in.

  • April 2021

    Catapultados

    For this game we wanted to get back to our roots: to make a fun game where every member can collaborate in the game desing. That's our thinking process, work as a family

Q3 2022

BattleCore Robots

This is our most ambitious project: a 3D arena game of highly competitive combat between cool customizable robots.
